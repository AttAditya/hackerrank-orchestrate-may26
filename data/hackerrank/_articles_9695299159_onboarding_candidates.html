Onboarding Candidates

Last updated 1 year ago

HackerRank is a leading technology hiring platform that enables you to hire skilled developers by conducting online assessments. Many companies use the HackerRank platform to conduct their campus hiring programs to select the right talent among them to suit your company's business requirements. The platform allows candidates to attend the hiring company's assessments and display their coding and technical skills to potential employers.

It is a good practice to familiarize your Candidates with the HackerRank platform before your hiring process begins. Communicate with your candidates about how HackerRank assessments work, what they can expect, or how long it may take. These practices reduce any surprises for your candidates and help maintain a positive atmosphere during each step of the hiring process. This article will help you with a few important aspects to consider in making the hiring process successful. 

Creating Tests

You can create tests in HackerRank based on a specific role, or you can even choose to customize your tests to suit your company’s hiring requirements. In role-based tests, you can generate Tests by including skill-mapped questions from the HackerRank library for HackerRank-created roles. In customized tests, you can build a Custom Test to include questions that assess candidates for a specific job role and their experience and skill level. You can also include Questions from the HackerRank built-in Library or your company library.

Invitation Templates

When numerous candidates participate in an event, it is important to keep them informed about the process. We have prepared an invitation email template comprising all the details a candidate needs to know before attempting the test. We recommend that you use this template or create your own, which reflects the idea of keeping candidates informed. Refer to Creating Email Template to learn how to create your templates.

Preparation Checklist

Your Candidates' experience is a key factor in hiring. Before or as part of inviting your Candidates to take up Tests using HackerRank, you can:

  • Recommend the HackerRank Sample Test to your Candidates. Candidates can find the sample test on their login page. 

  • Suggest that they familiarize themselves with the HackerRank environment and its various capabilities for writing and executing code and solving your challenges.

  • Give Browser recommendations for the best experience.

  • Ask them to check their network coverage before taking the Test. Provide them with an Internet compatibility check to check their connectivity.

  • If it is a proctored test, ask them to sit in a well-lit area and face straight at the camera.

  • Inform them to log in at least a few minutes before the test expiration time so that they have enough time to fill out the confirmation form.   

  • Show the invigilation methods you used to monitor these online assessments.

  • Provide precise instructions on how to log in to the Test and give credentials if necessary. 

  • Explain how the Candidates will be assessed on these Tests. 

Familiarizing your Candidates with your HackerRank hiring platform also gives them the impression of your Company's goal to hire only the best talent, thereby making your Candidates feel excited to attend your Tests and motivated to perform better.

Support 

We have created a dedicated support page for candidates. You can inform the candidates about the page and ask them to have a look to familiarize themselves before taking the test. Also, they can go through the FAQs to clear their doubts. Candidates can reach out to support@hackerrank.com for technical issues.
