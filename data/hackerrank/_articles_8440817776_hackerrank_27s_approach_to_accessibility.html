HackerRank's Approach to Accessibility

Last updated 1 year ago

At HackerRank, accessibility is an integral part of our software development process. We are committed to creating inclusive experiences that empower everyone to access and use our platform effortlessly. From the earliest design decisions to final testing, we ensure our products meet accessibility standards and provide a seamless user experience. We create products that work for everyone by identifying and addressing barriers early.

Proactive Testing and Audits

  • Regular Audits: We conduct internal and external audits, ensuring alignment with accessibility standards.

  • Tools and Technology: We leverage tools like Axe for automated testing & NVDA and Voiceover for manual testing.

Prioritizing and Addressing Issues

Accessibility is an ongoing process. Issues are prioritized based on their impact, ensuring critical challenges are resolved first.

  • Critical: Block access to core functionality. This is resolved with the highest urgency.

  • Serious: Significantly hinder access but allow alternative paths. These are prioritized right after critical issues.

  • Moderate and Low: These cause minor inconveniences or fail technical criteria without affecting usability. We address these as part of our long-term roadmap.

This approach ensures we address the most pressing needs while steadily improving our platform.

Third-Party Components

While we strive to address accessibility challenges proactively, we rely on certain third-party components for specific functionalities. Fixes for third-party components are often beyond our direct control, and hence, third-party components are excluded from our accessibility scope unless an accessible alternative is available.

Accommodation options

Limitations posed by third-party components or intricate technical constraints may prevent immediate resolution. In such cases, we provide in-product accommodation options and dedicated support to ensure users can effectively achieve their objectives.

Commitment to Continuous Improvement

We continuously evaluate and refine our processes to align with evolving accessibility standards and user needs.

  • Through each audit and review cycle, we identify areas for improvement, address potential gaps, and enhance usability.

  • By investing in tools, training, and internal processes, we prioritize accessibility in our continuously evolving products.


For inquiries or to access our latest Accessibility Conformance Reports, please contact us at accessibility@hackerrank.com.
